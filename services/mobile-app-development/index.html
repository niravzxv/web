Then, we’ll test each module for its required functionality before handing it over to our client once we're satisfied.
In what ways are cross-platform and native apps different? +
In comparison to cross-platform development, native application development is vastly different. A single app can run on both platforms thanks to cross-platform development. Native app development uses the Android and iOS ecosystems' default development languages and IDEs. Cross-platform apps that have been properly-developed work just as well as native apps, but they cost less time and money to produce.
When launching our app, which platform should we aim for, Android or iOS? +
Businesses may have previously chosen to design apps that only supported a particular platform. Nowadays, both Android and iOS have a significant market share that most companies develop apps that work on both platforms. If you're targeting an Android or iOS user, you must also research which operating system they prefer.
