Website Testing Services

We offer top-notch website testing services to ensure your website is ready for launch. Our experienced team of QA engineers will test your website's functionality and performance, ensuring that it performs flawlessly in all browsers, devices, and operating systems.

We also perform Indiability and speed tests to make sure that you're able to access your site from any device or screen size without issue. With the help of website testing services, we can examine how user-friendly your website is, see how well it functions, and determine if it can sustain high traffic volumes and prevent site data from cyberattacks.

Mobile Application Testing

With our mobile application testing services, we make sure that your mobile apps are ready for launch. We evaluate mobile applications for functionality, compatibility, Indiability, performance, scalability, and security. We also perform manual reviews of the app's functionality to ensure that it's ready for use by users who aren't familiar with mobile apps.

Our mobile app testers have extensive knowledge in testing applications for all major technologies, and platforms like Android and iOS. We aim to help companies save time, money, and resources by identifying bugs in their applications before they become major problems.

Contact us

Functional Testing Services

At Shivay infotech, functional testing is conducted at various granularity levels with the intent of ensuring that the software meets entirely with all its functional requirements. Both types of manual and automated testing services are available here. We will recommend the best method for automating functional testing efforts in your project based on the specifications of your project.

Our team of experts will perform tests on all aspects of your software's functionality and performance, including business logic and user experience/interaction. We guarantee complete transparency and control over the testing procedure by providing an extensive set of test documents.

Contact us

Regression Testing Services

Our Regression testing services are designed to ensure that recent code modifications made during software development and evolution have not negatively impacted the functionality of the product as it currently exists.

Our team of software testers conducts feasibility analysis to see whether automating regression testing is practical to lower test automation expenses. Once automation feasibility is established, only then do we move on with building a testing automation plan. We also constantly reduce the cost of automation throughout the project, for example, by prioritizing the functionality to automate and reusing the test cases that have already been created.

Contact us

Performance Testing Services

We offer a wide range of load and performance testing services to ensure that your software performs well under different conditions like high traffic volumes, low bandwidth, etc. We use various tools like load generators and simulation tools to simulate real-life user behavior on a website or application so that we can identify bottlenecks or slowdowns in the performance of your site which may hamper user experience or cause downtime on the server.

The cost of rectifying a performance issue increases with time. Therefore, we begin testing your software from the planning stage so that we can go into the development stage with confidence and obtain the most ROI possible from your solution.

Contact us
